Navigation Tips

  • Scroll / Swipe up to zoom in. Scroll / Swipe down to zoom out.
  • Double tap or press the Enter key to zoom in on touch devices.
  • Arrow Up and Arrow Down keys move between generations. Number keys jump directly to highlighted siblings.
  • When viewing the parents, tap a name tag to jump to that sibling’s branch.
