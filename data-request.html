Data Request

Use this page to request access, correction, deletion review, consent-related review, or another privacy-related action regarding personal information connected to Tomb of Light’s website or future platform services.

Submitting this form does not guarantee a specific outcome. Tomb of Light may need to verify identity, confirm authority, or request more information before completing certain requests.
What you can request

Request types

  • Access to certain personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion or removal review
  • Review of consent-related concerns
  • Objection or limitation request where applicable
  • Minor-related or family-sensitivity concerns
Before you submit

Important notes

  • Provide enough detail for the issue to be identified clearly
  • Do not include unnecessary highly sensitive information
  • Be prepared to verify identity or authority if needed
  • Use a valid email address for follow-up communication
