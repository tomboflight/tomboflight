About Vault Files
Vault files are private legacy media — voice messages, video messages, and protected archives — stored securely under your account. Only upload files you have the lawful right to submit. Do not upload Social Security cards, full Social Security numbers, payment card information, or other sensitive information not required for this workflow.
Vault files are not used in cinematic production by default. They remain private to you or your co-owner unless you explicitly change the privacy scope. Uploads may be reviewed for security, fraud prevention, compliance, and service fulfillment. Uploading a file does not guarantee delivery or legal validation.
For portraits and family photos, use Portrait Uploads. For birth certificates, marriage records, or other verification documents, use Verification Uploads.
