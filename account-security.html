Account Security

Password recovery and protected account changes.

Tomb of Light can issue secure reset requests, accept one-time reset links, and let signed-in users change their password directly inside the portal.

Reset Request

Enter the email attached to your Tomb of Light account. If it exists, a secure password reset link will be emailed to you.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.

Reset Password

Enter a strong new password to finish securing your account.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.

Change Password

Sign in to change your password from inside the protected workspace.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.
