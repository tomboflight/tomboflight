03

Launch Controls

Minting and reveal behavior stays controlled by policy.

The platform separates private workspace production from public collectible actions and keeps non-GA automation clearly labeled.

Controlled Minting

Collectible minting is a separate production step unless explicitly included in package scope. Private vault materials are not minted by default, and only approved delivery-safe collectible assets are eligible.

Advanced production policy

Automatic mint execution is not live by default. Any auto-mint behavior requires package policy support, runtime enablement, and intentional operational approval.

Advanced production policy

Scheduled reveal automation is planned and currently private beta / in development. Automatic timed release is not live without a dedicated production executor.

Experience Flow

How the platform experience comes together.

Tomb of Light is designed as a guided process from family intake to final delivery, turning personal history into a structured digital legacy experience.

01 Intake

Family data and media gathering

Families begin by providing names, relationships, portraits, media, and family structure details needed to organize the lineage experience.

02 Structure

Lineage mapping and branch organization

Family records are organized into a clearer architecture that supports a visual, navigable, multi-generation experience.

03 Build

Viewer assembly and visual presentation

Portraits, branches, descendants, and narrative layers are assembled into a premium digital lineage experience families can explore.

04 Review

Guided revision and refinement

Families review the build, confirm structure, and refine the presentation before final delivery and access are completed.

05 Delivery

Private access and legacy handoff

Once approved, the family receives a completed legacy experience with guided access, secure delivery, and continuation options.

06 Continuity

Expansion across generations

The platform is built to support future additions, lineage growth, and ongoing family preservation over time.
