Where do you want to upload?

Choose the correct upload type below. Each path routes to a dedicated upload workspace with the correct entitlement checks, metadata, and review workflow for that file type.

If you are still completing intake, the Upload Plan step records what you intend to upload — it does not upload files. Return here after intake is complete to submit your actual files.

Upload Status Key
After uploading, files show one of these statuses: uploaded (received), pending review (awaiting admin review), approved (cleared), approved for cinematic use (cleared for viewer production), approved for verification (cleared as a verified record), saved to vault (stored in private vault), needs correction (resubmission required), or rejected (not accepted). Uploading does not guarantee approval, legal validation, or production use.
A — Cinematic Photos & Portraits

Portraits & Family Photos

Use this for portraits, family photos, couple photos, parent-child photos, visual memories, and images intended for cinematic viewer or digital legacy production.

✓

Founder portraits

Primary subject portraits for cinematic viewer production.

✓

Family group photos

Spouse, children, parents, and household family images.

✓

Visual memories

Supporting imagery for digital legacy production. JPG, PNG, or WEBP only.

After upload: status will show uploaded → pending review → approved for cinematic use when cleared.

B — Verification Documents

Verification Records

Use this for birth certificates, marriage records, adoption records, death certificates, obituaries, government IDs when required, and other supporting records needed to verify lineage or identity.

✓

Birth & death certificates

Official vital records for lineage verification.

✓

Marriage & adoption records

Legal records confirming family relationships.

✓

Obituaries & supporting documents

Supporting family records. PDF, JPG, PNG, or WEBP only.

Do not upload Social Security cards, full Social Security numbers, payment card numbers, or other sensitive information unless Tomb of Light specifically requests it for a defined review purpose.
After upload: status will show uploaded → pending review → approved for verification when the record is cleared.
Upload Verification Documents
C — Vault Files

Private Vault Files

Use this for private legacy storage — voice messages, video messages, future messages, protected archives, and family media that should remain private to you or your co-owner.

✓

Private voice messages

Audio messages stored privately in your vault. MP3, M4A, WAV, WebM, OGG.

✓

Private video messages

Video messages stored privately in your vault. MP4, WebM, MOV.

✓

Protected legacy media

Vault files are not used in cinematic production by default. Privacy scope is set at upload.

After upload: status will show saved to vault once the file passes security review. Vault files remain private unless you explicitly change the privacy scope.
