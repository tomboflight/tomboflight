Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Tomb of Light may use cookies and similar technologies to support website functionality, preferences, analytics, and security.

Privacy Choices Privacy Policy
1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files or browser-based identifiers that help a website remember information such as preferences, session settings, or other interaction details.

2. Why Cookies May Be Used

Essential purposes

  • Support basic site functionality
  • Maintain user preferences
  • Help protect the website and detect misuse
  • Support load balancing or infrastructure operations

Optional purposes

  • Measure general site usage
  • Understand performance or navigation patterns
  • Improve layout and user experience
  • Support limited analytics where permitted
3. Preference Storage

The current website may store cookie preference selections on your device through browser-based storage. This helps the site remember whether optional analytics choices were accepted or declined.

4. Essential and Optional Technologies

Some site technologies may be necessary for the website to function properly. Optional analytics or similar tools may be used only where permitted and subject to user choice where applicable.

5. Managing Your Choices

Users may manage cookie choices through the website banner where available, through the Privacy Choices page, and through browser settings that control cookies or related storage mechanisms.

Manage Privacy Choices
6. Browser Controls

Many browsers allow users to block, delete, or limit cookies. Doing so may affect how parts of the website function or remember preferences.

7. Updates

Tomb of Light may update this Cookie Policy if website operations, analytics practices, or legal expectations change. The most recent version will be posted on this page.
