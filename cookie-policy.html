Cookie Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026. This Cookie Policy explains how Tomb of Light uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, protect security, remember user preferences, and improve the user experience.

This page works together with the Privacy Policy and Privacy Choices pages to help users understand how cookies and preference tools operate.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as login status, preferences, or security settings.

Cookies do not usually contain personally identifiable information on their own, but they may be associated with information you provide elsewhere on the site.

2. Why we use cookies

Cookies may be used for purposes including:

  • Operating core site functionality
  • Maintaining security and fraud prevention systems
  • Remembering cookie or privacy choices
  • Maintaining session information
  • Improving performance and reliability
  • Understanding general site usage patterns where permitted

3. Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies
    Required for basic operation of the website and cannot be disabled.
  • Security cookies
    Help protect the platform from abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.
  • Preference cookies
    Remember user choices such as cookie consent or site preferences.
  • Analytics cookies (optional)
    May help understand general site usage if enabled with user permission.

4. Optional analytics cookies

Optional analytics technologies may be used to understand general site performance and visitor behavior. These tools are intended to operate only where permitted by law and where user preference allows.

Users may decline optional analytics cookies through the cookie banner or privacy choices tools.

5. Managing cookie preferences

When you first visit the website, you may see a cookie banner allowing you to accept or decline optional analytics cookies.

You may change your preference later by visiting the Privacy Choices page.

6. Browser controls

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through browser settings. You may choose to block or delete cookies using your browser controls.

Disabling some cookies may affect the functionality or performance of the site.

7. Third-party technologies

Some services supporting site operation may use cookies or similar technologies. These may include hosting providers, infrastructure services, or analytics tools.

Tomb of Light does not control third-party cookie behavior outside its own systems.

8. Updates to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time as technologies, legal requirements, or platform features evolve.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

If you have questions regarding cookies or privacy preferences, contact:
