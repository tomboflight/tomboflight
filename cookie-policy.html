Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Tomb of Light LLC uses cookies and similar technologies to operate our website and improve user experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, maintain sessions, and analyze traffic.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies
    Required for the website to function properly, such as maintaining login sessions and security settings.
  • Optional Cookies
    Used to improve user experience, performance insights, or analytics when you consent.

3. Privacy Signals

Tomb of Light respects browser privacy signals such as Global Privacy Control (GPC). If your browser sends a GPC signal, optional tracking cookies will not be activated.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookie preferences through the cookie banner when visiting the site or by adjusting your browser settings.

You can also review and update your preferences on the Your Privacy Choices page.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our services evolve or as laws change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
