State Privacy Notices

Depending on your state of residence, you may have additional rights. These notices supplement our Privacy Policy.

California (CPRA)

  • Right to know, access, correct, and delete personal information.
  • Right to limit use/disclosure of sensitive personal information.
  • We do not “sell” personal information as defined by CPRA.
  • Submit requests via Data Rights Request.

Texas Data Privacy & Security Act

  • Right to access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to opt out of targeted advertising or profiling (not used by default).
  • Submit requests via Data Rights Request.

Virginia / Colorado / Connecticut

  • Rights include access, correction, deletion, portability, and opt-out of targeted ads/profiling.
  • Submit requests via Data Rights Request.

Appeals

If we decline your request, you may appeal by replying to our decision email with “Privacy Request Appeal.” We will review and respond within required timelines.
