U.S. state privacy notices

State Privacy Notices

This page provides additional privacy notice information that may apply to residents of certain U.S. states depending on applicable law, the nature of their interaction with Tomb of Light, and the types of personal information involved.

This page is a general public-facing notice and does not guarantee that every listed right applies in every situation. Eligibility, scope, and exceptions may depend on applicable state law and the context of the request.

1. Overview

Residents of certain states may have privacy rights concerning access to, correction of, deletion of, or portability for certain personal information, as well as rights relating to the sale, sharing, or use of personal data for targeted advertising in particular circumstances.

Tomb of Light’s public-facing privacy direction is privacy-first and does not state that the company sells personal information in the traditional sense described by many privacy laws.

2. Categories of personal information we may collect

Depending on your interaction with the site or future services, we may collect categories such as:

  • Identifiers such as name, email address, or account details
  • Communications and request content
  • Internet or network activity information related to site operation
  • Preference data such as privacy or cookie choices
  • User-submitted family legacy content where applicable
  • Sensitive or family-related information only where relevant to services or requests

3. Purposes for collection and use

We may collect or use information for purposes including:

  • Providing, securing, maintaining, or improving the website or services
  • Managing user accounts and support requests
  • Handling privacy, security, correction, or deletion-related concerns
  • Protecting users, Tomb of Light, and the platform from misuse or fraud
  • Complying with law or lawful requests

4. Rights that may be available under certain state laws

Depending on your state of residence and applicable law, you may have rights such as:

  • The right to know or access certain personal information
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • The right to request deletion of certain personal information
  • The right to request portability of certain information
  • The right to opt out of certain processing activities where applicable
  • The right to appeal a denied request in certain circumstances

5. How to exercise your rights

To submit a privacy-related request, use the Data Request page or contact admin@tomboflight.com.

Tomb of Light may need to verify your identity or authority before acting on certain requests.

6. Appeals process

If Tomb of Light declines to take action on a request, and applicable law provides an appeal right, you may respond to the decision and request further review. We may request additional information necessary to evaluate the appeal.

7. Authorized agents

Where allowed by law, you may authorize another person to act on your behalf. Tomb of Light may require reasonable proof of the agent’s authority and may also require direct identity verification from the individual whose data is involved.

8. Sensitive information and family legacy context

Because Tomb of Light may involve family identity, lineage, memory, or legacy-related information, sensitive handling may be required in some cases. Tomb of Light’s platform direction is to avoid public on-chain exposure of sensitive family information and to use privacy-first treatment where applicable.

9. Non-discrimination

Tomb of Light does not intend to unlawfully discriminate against individuals for exercising applicable privacy rights.

10. California notice context

California residents may have rights under California privacy laws, including rights relating to access, deletion, correction, and certain opt-out choices, subject to eligibility, scope, and applicable exceptions.

11. Other state notice context

Residents of other states with privacy laws may also have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, portability, opt-out choices, or appeals, depending on the relevant law and circumstances.

12. Updates to this notice

Tomb of Light may update this page as laws, services, or practices evolve. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date or last-updated date.

13. Contact

For questions regarding this notice or applicable privacy rights, contact: admin@tomboflight.com

Need to submit a formal request?

Use the Data Request page if you want Tomb of Light to review a privacy-related request.
