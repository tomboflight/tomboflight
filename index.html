About Tomb of Light

Tomb of Light is a revolutionary digital family legacy vault powered by blockchain and NFT technology. Founded by Larry Robinson with Jennifer Wood, Leon Floyd, Marquis Floyd, and Toni Floyd, Tomb of Light transforms your family tree into an everlasting, secure digital archive. Unlike traditional ancestry services, we allow you to preserve your story through advanced portrait NFTs: when someone zooms into your eyes, they will see your descendants and lineage visually connected for generations to come.

We believe your legacy deserves more than paper trails or temporary online records. By leveraging blockchain technology, Tomb of Light ensures your family's essence and story are permanently secured, enabling you to connect your past, present, and future in a timeless digital vault that cannot be lost or erased.
