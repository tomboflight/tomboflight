About Tomb of Light

Tomb of Light LLC helps families preserve their legacy using blockchain and NFTs to create interactive, secure, and permanent family heritage records. Your portrait is the anchor: zoom in to reveal descendants; zoom out to visit ancestors.

We are currently onboarding families for our Genesis NFT Collection, allowing you to secure your family's legacy for generations.

Why Choose Tomb of Light?

  • Forever Linked: Each generation seamlessly adds their legacy, creating a perpetual family chain secured on the blockchain.
  • Interactive & Visual: A living, zoomable family tree you can explore together.
  • Private & Simple: We handle the tech; you control access with owner keys and family sub-keys.

What You Get

Living Family NFT. Begin with your portrait, then expand as your family grows.

Owner Access Control. Owner holds the master key; invite editors with sub-keys.

Durable Storage. Images and metadata can be anchored to decentralized storage.

Guided Onboarding. We support you through ID verification and setup.
