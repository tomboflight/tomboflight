What Tomb of Light creates

A Tomb of Light build is not just a file folder or a flat chart.

Tomb of Light is not primarily a research database. It is a private legacy platform for building, protecting, and experiencing family lineage across generations.

01

Private by default

This is a protected workspace model, not a public browsing environment for scattered family data.

02

Hybrid Platform + Guided Production

Customers receive software access, secure submission tools, structured review, and human-supported legacy building.

03

Structured Verification

Narrative legacy, verified records, protected uploads, and continuity support all live in one controlled system.

Private Household Vault

Private Household Vault

Digital Legacy Portrait and higher family packages include a Private Household Vault for protected photos, videos, voice recordings, important documents, family records, legacy letters, private instructions, and household-only materials.

Vault access is limited to the authorized household/account and approved keyholders. Supported higher-tier packages may include future-message and scheduled-reveal features through controlled release workflows.
