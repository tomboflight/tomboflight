About Tomb of Light

Tomb of Light LLC helps families preserve their legacy using blockchain and NFTs to create interactive, secure, and permanent family heritage records.

We are currently onboarding families for our Genesis NFT Collection, allowing you to secure your family's legacy for generations.

What You Get

With Tomb of Light, you receive a beautifully designed, interactive family NFT that grows as your family grows. By zooming into your portrait, your children and their stories appear inside. When your children inherit the NFT, it expands to include their children, forming a living, visual family tree that links generations together forever on the blockchain. This creates an eternal, interactive record of your family's legacy that can never be lost or destroyed.

Why Choose Tomb of Light?

  • Forever Linked: Each generation seamlessly adds their legacy, creating a perpetual family chain secured forever on the blockchain.
  • Interactive & Visual: Unlike charts or photos, your NFT is a living, zoomable family tree you can explore with your loved ones.
  • Easy & Private: We handle all the blockchain and NFT complexities, ensuring your legacy remains private, secure, and under your ownership.
