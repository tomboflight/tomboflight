Blockchain / NFT Minting

Digital collectible minting is controlled, not default.

  • Blockchain / NFT minting is a separate production step unless explicitly included in the purchased package.
  • Minting covers collectible preparation, metadata setup, mint execution, and applicable blockchain network fees.
  • Private vault materials are not minted by default.
  • Only approved delivery-safe collectible assets are eligible for minting.
  • Included mint paths: Digital Legacy Portrait, Household Foundation, Heirloom Legacy Tree, Legacy Plus (1), Family Estate Concierge (up to 3).
  • Command Structure Network minting is opt-in and fee-based (service + network), not included by default.
  • Automatic mint execution is not live by default; runtime flags, approvals, and fee readiness can still block execution.
  • Scheduled reveal automation and automatic timed release remain planned/private beta unless a live executor is confirmed.

Founder Story

Why Tomb of Light was built.

Tomb of Light began with a deeper question: what happens to family history when memories are left scattered across devices, folders, and time?

This platform was created to give families and organizations a more meaningful way to preserve lineage, honor memory, and build a digital legacy that feels alive rather than archived.

Tomb of Light is not just about storing media. It is about protecting inheritance, identity, command structure, and continuity through a premium digital experience built to last.
