Private Household Vault

Private Household Vault

Digital Legacy Portrait and higher family packages include a Private Household Vault designed to protect and preserve the family’s most important memories, records, and future messages.

The vault may hold photos, videos, voice recordings, important documents, family records, legacy letters, private instructions, and household-only materials. Vault access is limited to the authorized household/account and approved keyholders.

Supported higher-tier packages may also include future-message and scheduled-reveal features, allowing selected private materials to remain hidden until a chosen release date through controlled release workflows.

Family-first privacy Controlled release workflows Authorized keyholder access

Private Household Vault included with Digital Legacy Portrait, Household Foundation, Heirloom Legacy Tree, Legacy Plus, and Family Estate Concierge.

Command Structure Network includes Organization Records Vault access for organizational structure, leadership records, nodes, levels, and command documentation. It is not a household vault package by default.

Founder Story

Why Tomb of Light was built.

Tomb of Light began with a deeper question: what happens to family history when memories are left scattered across devices, folders, and time?

This platform was created to give families and organizations a more meaningful way to preserve lineage, honor memory, and build a digital legacy that feels alive rather than archived.

Tomb of Light is not just about storing media. It is about protecting inheritance, identity, command structure, and continuity through a premium digital experience built to last.
