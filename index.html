About Tomb of Light

Tomb of Light LLC is dedicated to preserving family legacies, stories, and historical achievements on the blockchain, ensuring your family heritage is secured forever.

Founded by Larry Robinson II, Tisha, Tony, Leon, and Marquise, Tomb of Light combines blockchain technology with heritage preservation. Our mission is to empower families to immortalize their stories using secure NFTs stored permanently on-chain.

We are launching the Genesis NFT Collection, onboarding families into our vault system, and creating a timeless, secure legacy environment. This is not just NFTs; this is a new era of heritage preservation.
