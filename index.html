How the platform is structured

A technical foundation for digital family legacy.

Tomb of Light combines secure data handling, premium presentation, and future-ready ownership architecture in one platform direction.

1. Family record layer

Portraits, stories, lineage data, and legacy materials are intended to live in protected, platform-managed environments.

2. Permission layer

Living individuals, minors, and sensitive materials should be handled through authority, consent, and access rules.

3. Ownership layer

Future blockchain-backed ownership systems are intended to reference ownership without putting private records on public networks.

4. Continuity layer

The long-term goal is generational continuity, allowing family history to remain organized, protected, and transferable.
