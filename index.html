About Tomb of Light

Tomb of Light LLC is a pioneering NFT project dedicated to preserving family legacies, stories, and historical achievements on the blockchain, ensuring they can never be lost or erased.

Founded by Larry Robinson II and partners Tisha, Tony, Leon, and Marquise, Tomb of Light combines technology and family heritage preservation. Our mission is to empower families to immortalize their lineage, achievements, and stories using secure NFTs stored forever on-chain.

Our task as a team is to launch the Genesis NFT collection, establish vault services for families, and create a platform where each family can securely manage and pass down their stories for generations.

What makes Tomb of Light unique is its commitment to privacy, legacy preservation, and cultural continuity using blockchain technology—moving beyond collectibles, we are creating true digital monuments for families.

We are looking to onboard families worldwide into our system, providing them with secure NFT vaults and onboarding them into Web3, preserving their legacies in a way never done before. Get ready for the big launch—join the waitlist and secure your family’s place in the Tomb of Light.
