What Tomb of Light Is

Understanding the product and what makes it different.

What is Tomb of Light?

Tomb of Light is a private digital legacy platform designed to preserve and explore family lineage through structured visual experiences, guided family architecture, protected records, and a premium multi-generation viewing experience.

Is Tomb of Light just an NFT project?

No. Tomb of Light is not centered on hype alone. The broader model is about family legacy, private stewardship, lineage structure, and long-term continuity. Any ownership-related layer is meant to support the platform without making private family material publicly exposed.

Is this a family tree?

It includes family tree structure, but it is more than a flat chart. Tomb of Light is built as a visual lineage experience where portraits, branches, descendants, and family memory can be explored in a more meaningful way.

Comparison Questions

How Tomb of Light compares to other family history companies.

Are you like Ancestry.com?

No. Tomb of Light is not designed to be a direct replacement for a traditional genealogy search database. Services like Ancestry are centered on historical records, document discovery, and broad family research. Tomb of Light is centered on a private, premium, visual legacy experience designed around family presentation, continuity, memory, and stewardship.

So what makes Tomb of Light different?

Tomb of Light is designed to feel more personal, more visual, and more intentional. Instead of only listing names and records, it is built to help families preserve portraits, relationships, lineage structure, and legacy in an experience that feels alive.

Why would someone choose Tomb of Light over other options?

Because some families want more than a database, a folder, or a static chart. They want a legacy experience that honors identity, memory, and family continuity in a format worthy of the people it represents.

Buying and Value

Why buying a Tomb of Light experience can matter.

Why is buying important?

Because family legacy is one of the few things that cannot be replaced once it is lost. Buying a Tomb of Light experience is an investment in preserving memory, identity, lineage, and continuity for the people who come after you.

Who is Tomb of Light for?

It is for individuals, parents, memorial families, legacy-minded households, and larger family branches that want a more meaningful and lasting way to preserve history.

Why not just keep photos in a folder?

Privacy and Blockchain

How private family material is meant to be handled.

Will private family data be public?

Tomb of Light is designed around private stewardship, careful access, and respectful handling of family materials. Sensitive stories, portraits, and related records should not be exposed casually.

Will private family data be on-chain?

The intended direction is that private family stories, media, and sensitive materials remain protected in controlled systems rather than being openly exposed through public layers.

Why not put everything on a blockchain?

Because unrestricted public exposure is not appropriate for sensitive family legacy material. Tomb of Light is built around dignity, privacy, and continuity rather than careless visibility.

Family Participation

Questions about living family members, minors, and inclusion.

Can I include living family members?

Inclusion involving living individuals should be handled respectfully and, where appropriate, with consent, authorization, or another valid basis depending on the circumstances.

What about children or minors?

If minors are included in a family experience, parent or guardian permission should apply, along with heightened privacy expectations and more careful handling.

Can information be corrected later?

Tomb of Light is intended to support correction-aware stewardship so inaccurate or disputed information can be reviewed and handled responsibly.
