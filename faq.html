Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tomb of Light?
A private, interactive “living family tree” NFT secured on the blockchain.

How does it work?
We build a zoomable tree around your portrait: zoom in to descendants, zoom out to parents. Access is controlled by the owner.

Is it private?
Yes. We restrict access, honor opt-out signals, and keep documents encrypted.

What documents do you collect?
Government ID, birth certificate, and proof of residence for identity/descendant verification.

Do you sell or share my data?
No. If any optional analytics qualifies as “sharing,” you can opt out via Your Privacy Choices. We honor supported Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals.

How do I get started?
Join the waitlist on the homepage; we’ll guide your onboarding.
