Commitment to Privacy, Security, and Compliance

  • Privacy Laws. We align our practices with applicable laws, including the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA). Where relevant, we address rights under other U.S. state privacy laws and GDPR for covered users.
  • Data Minimization. We collect only what is reasonably necessary for identity verification and service delivery.
  • Security Measures. Encryption in transit, restricted access, and monitoring aimed at protecting sensitive documents and images.
  • User Rights. Access, correction, deletion, and opt-out mechanisms are described in our Privacy Policy and State Privacy Notices.
  • Consumer Protection. We follow applicable consumer protection laws, including the Texas Deceptive Trade Practices Act.
  • AML / KYC. Where identity verification is required to prevent fraud or misuse, we follow AML/KYC best practices appropriate to our services.
  • Non-Securities Statement. Tomb of Light NFTs are for legacy preservation and personal storytelling. They are not investment products and do not grant profit-sharing.
