How these choices work

When you save your choices on this page, your settings are stored locally in your browser on this device. If you clear browser storage, switch devices, or use a different browser, these settings may not carry over.

Need to submit a formal rights request?

If you need to request access, correction, deletion review, or another privacy-law-related action, use the formal Data Request page instead of relying on browser-stored preference settings alone.
