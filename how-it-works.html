How It Works (High-Level)

  • Your Portrait. The experience begins with a single portrait that represents the family owner.
  • Zoom Navigation. Zooming provides a private, visual way to explore close relatives in your family tree.
  • Owner Keys. The owner controls access. Optional sub-keys allow trusted family editors.
  • Durable Storage. Images and metadata can be anchored to decentralized storage for persistence.
  • NFT Anchor. A non-financial NFT anchors your private family page and access controls.

We purposely share only high-level information publicly. Details about interfaces and data models are provided to customers during onboarding.
