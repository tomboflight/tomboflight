How It Works

  1. Purchase & Create: Choose a plan and start your family NFT.
  2. Verify: Upload a government ID, birth certificate, and proof of residence.
  3. Build: We format your portrait and initialize your zoomable family tree.
  4. Mint: Your NFT is minted and your private family page is activated.
  5. Grow: Add descendants over time; invite family with access keys.
