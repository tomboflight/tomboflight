Privacy policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Tomb of Light LLC may collect, use, protect, retain, and review personal information in connection with its website, policy pages, contact pathways, and future family legacy platform services.

Tomb of Light is being built as a privacy-first family legacy platform. Some described platform features may still be in development, but this policy is intended to describe the company’s public-facing privacy direction now and as services expand.

1. Who we are

Tomb of Light LLC is building a secure digital family legacy platform designed to help preserve family portraits, lineage information, stories, and related legacy materials in a controlled environment.

Contact email: admin@tomboflight.com

2. Information we may collect

Depending on how you interact with the site or future services, we may collect:

  • Contact information such as your name and email address
  • Account information such as login credentials or profile details
  • Communications you send to us through forms or email
  • Preference information such as cookie or privacy choices
  • Technical and device information necessary for site operation and security
  • Family legacy content you choose to submit, where applicable
  • Rights request or correction request details

3. How we may use information

We may use information for purposes such as:

  • Operating, maintaining, and securing the website or platform
  • Providing account access, support, or request handling
  • Responding to policy, privacy, or security concerns
  • Reviewing correction, deletion, or access requests
  • Improving site performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Preventing misuse, fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity
  • Complying with legal obligations or protecting rights and safety

4. Privacy-first treatment of family legacy content

Tomb of Light’s platform direction is to avoid placing sensitive family information directly on public blockchains. Family stories, media, records, and similar legacy materials are intended to remain in controlled systems rather than being exposed as public on-chain content.

  • Private family materials are intended to remain off-chain
  • Ownership records should avoid exposing sensitive personal details
  • Family legacy content may receive heightened protection due to its sensitive nature

5. Family participation, consent, and accuracy

Family identity information can be sensitive. Where users submit or manage family-related content, they are expected to do so responsibly and with appropriate authority, permission, or lawful basis where required.

Living individuals should not be included in a misleading, deceptive, harmful, or unauthorized way. Inaccurate, outdated, disputed, or improperly submitted information may be subject to correction, review, or removal.

6. Children and minor-related information

Tomb of Light does not intend for children to independently use the platform in a way that bypasses appropriate adult oversight. If minor-related family content is ever included, parent or guardian permission is expected, and such material should receive heightened protection.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

The site uses essential technologies required for core site operation and may offer optional analytics or preference tools where permitted. Optional analytics should not be enabled unless the user permits them where applicable.

For more information, see the Cookie Policy and Your Privacy Choices page.

8. How we may share information

We may share information only where reasonably necessary, including:

  • With service providers supporting hosting, operations, security, or communications
  • To comply with law, regulation, legal process, or lawful requests
  • To protect rights, safety, platform integrity, or users from misuse
  • As part of a business transaction where permitted by law and policy
  • With your direction or consent where applicable

Tomb of Light does not state that it sells personal information in the traditional sense described by many privacy laws.

9. Data retention

We may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including service operation, security, legal compliance, dispute handling, and recordkeeping. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, the sensitivity involved, and whether the data is needed for active services or legal obligations.

10. Security

Tomb of Light uses a privacy-first security direction intended to protect sensitive information through controlled storage, transport protection, restricted access, and operational safeguards appropriate to the stage of service development.

No system can promise absolute security. Please review our Security page for more information.

11. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location and relationship to the site or future services, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or review of certain personal information. You may also be able to request limitations on specific processing activities where applicable by law.

To submit a request, use the Data Request page or contact admin@tomboflight.com.

12. Identity verification for requests

Before fulfilling certain privacy or rights requests, Tomb of Light may need to verify your identity or confirm your authority to act on behalf of another person. We may request enough information to reasonably verify the request while trying to minimize unnecessary collection.

13. Appeals and follow-up

If a request cannot be fully honored, Tomb of Light may provide an explanation where appropriate and may offer a follow-up path, appeal path, or additional review depending on the nature of the request and applicable law.

14. External links

The site may contain links to third-party sites or services. Tomb of Light is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites outside its control. Review those services separately before sharing information.

15. Changes to this policy

Tomb of Light may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the platform evolves, legal requirements change, or operational practices mature. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date or last-updated date.

16. Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, contact: admin@tomboflight.com

Review the related policy pages.
