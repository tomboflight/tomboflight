Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2025

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Tomb of Light LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, email, and similar details for waitlist and onboarding.
  • Identity & Verification Documents: Government-issued ID, birth certificate, and proof of residence for identity and descendant verification.
  • Account/Usage Information: Limited technical data (e.g., device type, basic interaction logs). Optional analytics only with your consent.

How We Use Information

  • To onboard you and verify your identity and descendant claims.
  • To create, manage, and support your family NFT and private page.
  • To communicate with you, including service and security notices.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

Sensitive Documents

Identity and proof documents are encrypted in transit and at rest and accessible only to authorized personnel for verification purposes. We do not use these documents for advertising.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may disclose information to service providers who assist with secure storage, payment processing, identity verification, or regulatory compliance, under contractual obligations. We may disclose information if required by law.

Cookies & Opt-Out Signals

We use essential cookies to operate our site. Optional analytics/ads require your consent. We honor supported universal opt-out signals, such as Global Privacy Control (GPC). Manage preferences via Your Privacy Choices.

Your Rights

Depending on your state or country, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your data, and to opt out of sale/sharing and targeted advertising where applicable. See State Privacy Notices. You can also contact us to exercise rights.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information, including encryption, access controls, and least-privilege practices.

Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law, and then securely delete or anonymize it.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Effective Date” reflects the latest version.
