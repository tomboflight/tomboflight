Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tomb of Light may collect, use, protect, and manage information through its public website and related communications as the platform evolves.

Privacy Choices Data Requests
1. Scope

This policy applies to the public website and related interactions.

This policy applies to information collected through the Tomb of Light website, forms, request-access workflows, and related support or compliance communications unless a different notice is provided for a specific service or program.

2. Information We May Collect

Information you provide

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Messages submitted through website forms
  • Request-access or support inquiry information
  • Any other details you choose to provide voluntarily

Information collected automatically

  • Basic device and browser information
  • IP address or approximate location signals
  • Website usage and interaction data
  • Cookie or similar preference information where used
3. How Information May Be Used

Tomb of Light may use collected information to operate the website, respond to inquiries, evaluate request-access interest, improve user experience, protect the website, and support compliance, security, or operational needs.

  • Provide and improve the public website
  • Respond to support, privacy, or compliance requests
  • Maintain security and detect misuse
  • Review onboarding or access-related interest
  • Meet legal or operational obligations
4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Tomb of Light may use essential website technologies and may use optional analytics tools where permitted. Cookie preferences may be stored on the user’s device through browser-based mechanisms.

5. Sharing of Information

Tomb of Light does not describe the public website as a system for selling personal information in the ordinary sense. Information may be shared with service providers, hosting providers, security providers, legal advisors, or authorities where reasonably needed for operations, compliance, or protection.

  • Website hosting and infrastructure support
  • Security and fraud-prevention services
  • Professional advisors and legal obligations
  • Business protection and operational continuity needs
6. Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the website, respond to requests, maintain records, protect the business, or comply with applicable obligations.

Depending on location and applicable law, users may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or other information about how their data is handled.

Tomb of Light may update this Privacy Policy as website features, operational practices, or legal expectations change. Updated versions will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
