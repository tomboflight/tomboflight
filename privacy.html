Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025

Tomb of Light LLC (“Tomb of Light,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information entrusted to us.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact: name, email, and preferences you provide.
  • Family Materials (optional): photos, stories, documents, and metadata you choose to upload.
  • Consent Records: confirmations for every living individual included in a project; parent/guardian consent for minors.
  • Technical: device/browser information, IP address, pages viewed, diagnostic logs.
  • Support: messages and attachments you submit to our team.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, personalize, and improve the Services.
  • Verify and document consent for living individuals; handle parental consent for minors.
  • Operate your private legacy vault and project workflow.
  • Communicate about your account, security, updates, and service notices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3) Consent & Inclusion

  • Consent is required for inclusion of all living individuals.
  • Parent or guardian consent is required for minors.
  • Sensitive information is intended to remain private by default.

4) Blockchain & NFTs

  • We do not place personal family data on public blockchains.
  • NFTs function as certificates, access keys, or legacy anchors, not investment products.
  • If consent is withdrawn, off-chain materials may be restricted or removed where applicable.

5) Security

  • Encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Role-based access controls and activity logging.
  • Access limited to authorized users according to role.

6) Your Rights & Choices

  • You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data where applicable.
  • You may withdraw consent for inclusion at any time.
  • You may submit requests through our Data Rights Request page.

7) Cookies & Privacy Signals

  • We use essential cookies to operate the website.
  • Optional analytics are off by default unless you opt in.
  • Where supported, we honor privacy preference signals such as Global Privacy Control.
  • You can manage preferences on Your Privacy Choices.

8) Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain records, and satisfy applicable legal obligations.

9) Children

We include minors only with verified parent or guardian consent. If you believe a child has been included without proper authorization, contact us immediately.

10) Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@tomboflight.com
General contact: admin@tomboflight.com
