Platform Roadmap

The Tomb of Light platform is being built in phases to ensure security, privacy, and long-term generational preservation of family history.

Phase 1 — Foundation

  • Secure website infrastructure
  • Privacy and compliance policies
  • Security framework and encryption planning
  • Family legacy concept prototype

Phase 2 — Platform Core

  • User accounts
  • Family vault creation
  • Encrypted media storage
  • Family member structure

Phase 3 — Ownership Layer

  • NFT ownership layer
  • Secure vault referencing
  • Ownership transfer mechanisms

Phase 4 — Immersive Family Tree

  • 3D generational navigation
  • Timeline history exploration
  • Interactive family stories

Phase 5 — Global Platform

  • Mobile app
  • VR family exploration
  • multi-generation family networks
