Refunds, Delivery & Support Policy

Tomb of Light projects are guided digital production services. This page summarizes support routing, delivery timing expectations, cancellation and refund review, and maintenance boundaries. See Terms for complete details.

Support Channels

How to contact Tomb of Light.

  • For general customer support, contact support@tomboflight.com or call (346) 805-4305. Typical response time: 1–2 business days.
  • Billing questions: billing@tomboflight.com
  • Privacy questions and data requests: privacy@tomboflight.com or Data Requests
  • Legal routing: legal@tomboflight.com
  • Compliance routing: compliance@tomboflight.com
  • Security concerns: security@tomboflight.com
Delivery

When delivery timelines begin.

Delivery timelines begin after completed intake and required materials are received, not immediately at checkout.

  • Estimated timelines begin after completed intake and required materials are received.
  • Customers may be contacted for corrections, missing records, or approval before final delivery.
  • Larger household, network, estate, or organization projects may require additional review time.
Refunds and Cancellations

Refund and cancellation requests are reviewed by project stage.

  • Before production begins: Customers may request cancellation review by contacting support@tomboflight.com or calling (346) 805-4305. If intake, workspace setup, review, verification, design, or production work has not begun, Tomb of Light may approve a refund to the original payment method when possible.
  • After workspace setup or intake begins: Once a private workspace, package setup, intake review, upload review, verification review, design planning, or production preparation has begun, refunds may be limited because custom production work has started.
  • After custom production begins: Once custom production, review, verification, design, viewer preparation, certificate preparation, collectible preparation, or delivery work has begun, payments are generally non-refundable except where required by law or where Tomb of Light determines that a correction, credit, or partial resolution is appropriate.
  • After delivery: Completed and delivered custom digital production work is generally non-refundable except where required by law. Support, corrections, maintenance, or package-compatible updates may be handled through the applicable support or maintenance process.
  • Approved refunds: Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method when possible. Processing times may depend on the payment provider and customer bank.
  • Company-caused inability to deliver: If Tomb of Light determines that it cannot provide the purchased service within package scope, Tomb of Light may offer a corrected delivery plan, substitution, credit, or refund review as appropriate.
  • Chargebacks and disputes: Customers are encouraged to contact support@tomboflight.com before filing a dispute so Tomb of Light can review project stage, payment status, and production history.

To request cancellation or refund review, contact support@tomboflight.com or call (346) 805-4305. See Terms for complete details.

Maintenance Boundaries

What maintenance covers and does not cover.

  • Maintenance is separate from a new build or package upgrade.
  • Maintenance supports access continuity, storage continuity, retrieval support, and package-compatible platform updates.
  • Maintenance does not include new genealogy research, new branch creation outside purchased scope, major redesigns, or unpurchased add-ons.
Privacy, Sharing, and Minting Boundaries

Private by default, controlled sharing, controlled minting.

  • Private vault uploads, family records, verification materials, workspace data, and protected family materials are not public by default.
  • Only approved delivery-safe materials, viewer links, certificates, or customer-approved presentation assets may be shared.
  • Collectible minting is controlled by package eligibility, customer approval, and production readiness.
  • Private vault materials are not minted by default.
  • Minting is not automatically triggered by checkout, upload, or intake.
  • Scheduled reveal automation is not publicly live unless a dedicated production executor and approval controls are active.
Customer Promise

Commitment to approval-based publication and minting.

Tomb of Light will not publish, mint, or publicly share private family materials without customer approval.
