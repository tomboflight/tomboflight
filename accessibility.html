Inclusive Access

Accessibility

Tomb of Light is committed to improving website usability and inclusive access over time so more people can navigate and use the public site effectively.

Effective date: March 26, 2026.

Commitment

Tomb of Light aims to improve conformance with WCAG 2.2 Level AA for public-facing content.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. Tomb of Light aims to improve public-facing website content in line with recognized web accessibility practices, including goals consistent with WCAG 2.2 Level AA where reasonably feasible.

Current Priorities
1

Readable structure

Improve headings, landmarks, labels, and content structure to support clearer navigation and comprehension.

2

Keyboard access

Continue improving keyboard navigation, focus behavior, and skip-link access across public website components.

3

Compatibility

Work toward better compatibility with modern browsers and assistive technologies where reasonably feasible.

Need Help?

Report accessibility barriers so Tomb of Light can review them.

If you experience an accessibility barrier on the Tomb of Light website, please contact support@tomboflight.com and include the page or feature involved, a description of the issue, the assistive technology or browser used if known, and any screenshots or steps that may help reproduce the issue.

Third-Party Content

Some third-party tools, integrations, embedded content, or linked services may have accessibility limitations outside Tomb of Light’s direct control. Tomb of Light may continue working to improve accessible alternatives where reasonably possible.

Ongoing Review

Accessibility is not a one-time project. Tomb of Light may continue refining design, content, navigation, and interaction patterns as the website and platform mature.
