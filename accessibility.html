Inclusive Access

Accessibility

Tomb of Light is committed to improving website usability and inclusive access over time so more people can navigate and use the public site effectively.

Commitment

Tomb of Light aims to make the public website more accessible to users with a wide range of needs, technologies, and assistive tools.

Ongoing Improvements
1

Readable structure

Improve page structure, headings, and content organization to support easier navigation and comprehension.

2

Keyboard access

Continue improving keyboard navigation and focus behavior across public website components.

3

Compatibility

Work toward better compatibility with modern browsers and assistive technologies where reasonably feasible.

Feedback

If you experience an accessibility barrier on the Tomb of Light website, you should be able to report the issue through a listed support or compliance contact channel so it can be reviewed.

Evolving Standard

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. Tomb of Light may continue refining design, content, navigation, and interaction patterns as the website and platform mature.
