Accessibility commitment

Accessibility

Tomb of Light is committed to improving website accessibility so more people can access information about the platform, policies, and future services with dignity, clarity, and ease of use.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. As the website and platform evolve, Tomb of Light intends to continue reviewing usability, navigation, readability, and compatibility issues and to improve them over time.

1. Our goal

Tomb of Light aims to make its website and future platform experiences more accessible to users with varying needs, devices, and assistive technologies. This includes working toward clearer structure, readable content, usable navigation, and more consistent page behavior.

2. Accessibility areas of focus

  • Consistent navigation across pages
  • Readable text contrast and visual hierarchy
  • Keyboard-usable interactive elements where possible
  • Mobile-friendly layouts and responsive structure
  • Clear page titles, headings, labels, and links
  • Improved support for screen-reader-friendly markup over time

3. Current improvement direction

Tomb of Light is actively standardizing site layout, policy navigation, form structure, and mobile menu behavior. Accessibility improvements are part of that same effort and are expected to continue as features expand.

4. Reporting an accessibility issue

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, usability problem, or difficulty accessing content on the website, please report it so it can be reviewed.

Email: admin@tomboflight.com

5. What to include in your report

  • The page or feature where the issue occurred
  • A brief description of the problem
  • The device and browser you were using, if known
  • Any assistive technology involved, if relevant and you choose to share it

6. Ongoing review

Tomb of Light may review accessibility feedback, prioritize improvements, and adjust page structure, form behavior, navigation, and content design as part of normal site updates and platform hardening.

Need help with a page or form?

If a website feature is difficult to use, contact Tomb of Light and report the issue.
